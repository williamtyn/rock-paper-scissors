Play Rock, Paper, Scissors

How to play the game

The game has three possible outcomes: a draw, a win or a loss.
As a player you can choose if you want to play with the rock, the paper or the scissors.
You will be playing against the computer and best to three (3) rounds, wins the match.

The rules

Rock wins against scissors.
Scissors win against paper.
Paper wins against rock.
